About the Contributers

Mapping the Brain was created for computer science research at Connecticut College in order to test and apply the principles of educational web technology. The contrubiters are Craig Haber, a computer science and psychology double major, and Mikaela Marinis, a computer science major. All content was reviewed by the neuroscience professor Dr. Joseph Schroeder for accuracy. In addition to Dr. Schroeder, special thanks to Dr. William Tarimo, a professor of computer science, for his continuous guidance and educational technology expertise.

If you would like to learn more, please check out the FAQ page. Otherwise, you can contact Craig at chaber@conncoll.edu or Mikaela at mmarinis@conncoll.edu.
